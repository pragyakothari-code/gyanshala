About Gyanshala

Our community, our teachers, our Sunday home.

Our Mission

JVBNA Gyanshala is a Jain Sunday school rooted in the timeless values of ahimsa (non-violence), satya (truthfulness), and aparigraha (non-possessiveness). We bring together children and families from across New Jersey each Sunday to study Jain philosophy, scripture, and language in a warm, community-centred environment.

Our curriculum spans four Jainism tracks — Sanskaar, Gyan, Darshan, and Charitra — alongside Hindi language classes and weekly SOL or Yoga sessions. Every lesson is designed to help students connect ancient wisdom to their everyday lives, building character alongside knowledge.
